Meet the Hawks

It's that time of year again... BASEBALL SEASON!

The Raleigh Hawks are a group of young men from S. E. Raleigh and surrounding areas who love the game of Baseball! These players are respectful, display good sportsmanship and soar academically as well as on the field. The parents, supporters and dedicated coaching staff are doing everything in their power to keep our kids engaged in positive environments as these are very critical years in shaping the direction of their future. Baseball is a game that requires patience, emphasizes team play, and at times requires sacrifice as a individual for the good of the team. These are life lessons that these young men will use on and off the field. You develop a brotherhood with your teammates that can last a lifetime.
